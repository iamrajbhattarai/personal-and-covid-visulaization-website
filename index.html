Study at Kathmandu University

The teaching and learning environment at KU is very good. Teachers are qualified, infrastructures are better and each class is equipped with projector. The peaceful environment and natural aura in the university makes study much more efficient. Various courses of engienering, science and management are available here.

Natural Aura and Peaceful Environment

The university has hilly terrain and there are trees planted everywhere. Some section of the university seems like park or small forest. The chirping sound of the birds at the university premises provides soothing and relaxing sound making it perfect environment for studying. Moreover, a big beautifully designed library is available and readers can enjoy their time at peace.


Modern Infrastructures

The university has modern buildings which are well ventilated, receive proper lighting and are huge. Moreover, you can find automatic door in various buildings of the university. The bathrooms are well managed and contains automatic water taps and hand dryer. The roads are paved. Hostel and mesh are also made available.



Qualified Teachers and Well Equipped Classrooms

The university has managed to enroll highly qualified teachers who are sound of the modern technologies and modern way of teaching. Teachers engage students in various research works and show practical demonstration of the ongoing technologies around the world. The classroom are also well managed with proper seating made available and projector are also available which is exploited in teaching.


Bachelors in Geomatics Engineering

Bachelors in Geomatics Engineering offers various theory subjects and practicals. The first year is just foundation course consisting of pure science and mathematics. The other years undergo into core subjects like catrography, GIS, Spatial Database, Programming, Remote Sensing, Photohrammetry, etc.
